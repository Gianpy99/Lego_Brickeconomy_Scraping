Gandalf the Grey Minifigure

lor001 Gandalf the Grey wearing a wizard hat.
The LEGO Gandalf the Grey minifig was first introduced in 2012 in the set 9469 Gandalf Arrives and is currently estimated to be valued around £8 and an annual growth of about 8.7%. The minifigure has appeared in 4 sets across 2 themes, The Lord of the Rings being the first theme.

Gandalf the Grey is a wizard from J.R.R. Tolkien's Middle-earth legendarium, featured prominently in "The Hobbit" and "The Lord of the Rings" series.

Minifig Facts

The LEGO minifigure lor001 represents Gandalf the Grey, a character from J.R.R. Tolkien's Middle-earth legendarium, particularly in "The Hobbit" and "The Lord of the Rings." This minifigure features Gandalf wearing a wizard hat and holding a staff. LEGO released this minifigure in sets related to "The Lord of the Rings" series, starting in 2012. The minifigure includes details such as long grey hair, a grey beard, and printed robes that reflect the character’s appearance in the films.
Common description: Gandalf the Grey - wizard / witch hat
